تذكر الله

Remember Allah

Every moment of restraint is a victory of the soul over desire. The strength you show today echoes in eternity, and Allah sees every struggle, every choice to turn away from what harms the heart.

وَأَمَّا مَنْ خَافَ مَقَامَ رَبِّهِ وَنَهَى النَّفْسَ عَنِ الْهَوَىٰ فَإِنَّ الْجَنَّةَ هِيَ الْمَأْوَىٰ

"And as for he who feared the standing before his Lord and prevented the soul from [unlawful] inclination, then indeed, Paradise will be [his] abode."

— Surah An-Nazi'at (79:40-41)

In this moment, remember:

  • Your Lord is always watching with mercy and love
  • This urge will pass, but your choice echoes forever
  • Every "no" to temptation is a "yes" to your highest self
  • Paradise is built one righteous choice at a time

May Allah strengthen you in every moment of choice

A Dua for Strength

اللَّهُمَّ أَعِنِّي عَلَى ذِكْرِكَ وَشُكْرِكَ وَحُسْنِ عِبَادَتِكَ

"O Allah, help me to remember You, thank You, and worship You in the best manner."
